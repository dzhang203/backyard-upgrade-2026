# SMMC 27.18.090 — Rear Yards (R1 District)

**Source:** [https://law.cityofsanmateo.org/us/ca/cities/san-mateo/code/27.18.090](https://law.cityofsanmateo.org/us/ca/cities/san-mateo/code/27.18.090)
**Chapter:** 27.18 — One-Family Dwelling (R1) District Regulations

---

## Full Text (as extracted)

> "A rear yard of not less than fifteen (15) feet shall be provided; a rear yard not less than twenty-five (25) feet shall be provided for new construction above a single story."

---

## Summary & Relevance to Project

**Standard rear yard setback in R1 zones:**
- Single-story structures: **15 feet** minimum from the rear property line
- Multi-story new construction: **25 feet** minimum

**However**, the rear-one-third exemption in SMMC 27.18.100 explicitly exempts qualifying accessory structures from this rear yard setback requirement as well. Meaning a qualifying gazebo in the rear 1/3 of the lot can sit right at the rear fence line, not just the side fence.

**Not directly relevant to the project** as currently designed — the gazebo is planned near the side fence, not the rear fence. But useful context: even the rear fence is not a concern for setback purposes if the structure is in the rear 1/3 of the lot and meets height requirements.
