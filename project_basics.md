# Project Basics

**Location:** 318 24th Ave, San Mateo, California 94403
**Zoning:** R1-C (single-family residential, ~0.15 acre lot)

## Scope

Resurface parts of the backyard to increase usable area and add a covered seating area.

- **Gazebo:** Purple Leaf 10' × 16' Hardtop Gazebo (aluminum frame, galvanized steel double roof), sited near the east (side) fence at the back of the yard.
- **Gazebo flooring:** 18' × 12' porcelain paver field (2 ft × 2 ft, 2 cm, rectified-edge), dry-laid on compacted aggregate base, tight continuous joints with polymeric sand.
- **Gazebo drainage:** Rain chains at each of the four corner posts terminating into subsurface dry wells (geotextile-lined, #57 stone).
- **Surround:** Loose 1" Lynn Creek smooth river rock on all four sides of the paver field.
- **Fence proximity:** Roof line 3 ft from east fence; pavers 1.5 ft from fence; river rock fills the 1.5 ft gap.
- **Plants:** Fence-line planting with a curated native / oak-woodland palette (see `plants/`).
- **Retained:** Existing circular fire pit, blue Tuff shed, string lights, artificial turf lawn.
- **Removed:** 1–2 existing vegetable planters.
